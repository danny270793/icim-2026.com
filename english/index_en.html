VI Ecuadorian Mathematics Conference

The Ecuadorian Mathematics Conference (EMC) is an annual scientific event organized collaboratively by Ecuadorian university institutions, in association with the Ecuadorian Mathematical Society (EMS), the Mathematical Modeling Center (Mode-Mat), and Amarun.

The fundamental purpose of the EMC is to provide suitable environments for dissemination, analysis, reflection, and debate, with the aim of stimulating the advancement of both research and higher education in mathematics in Ecuador. The essence of the EMC lies in its contribution to the consolidation of strategic alliances among researchers, at both national and international levels. The EMC is not only a manifestation of scientific excellence but also a fundamental component for strengthening the academic and scientific community in mathematics, encouraging research and teaching.

This year, the VI Ecuadorian Mathematics Conference will be organized by the Mathematics Department of the Faculty of Sciences, at the Escuela Superior Politécnica de Chimborazo in the city of Riobamba, and will take place in November.
